What makes learning at Sri Barathi Vidyalaya an active, joyful process of discovery is that age is not a bar to shouldering responsibilities and taking initiative. Students seek out challenges, ask essential questions, and solve problems in inventive ways at every stage of their schooling.

The SBVians

Future readiness—emotional, cognitive and social

We recognise the achiever in every child, because awards can’t really capture a student’s invaluable individual traits. We celebrate every achievement, no feat is too small.

Our students are celebrated for their unique, self-directed initiatives or participating selflessly in another’s. These range from unusual personal creative endeavours (a book of poetry, for example) to those that affect citizens or take up public causes. To make these happen without programming is the greatest award of all.

Our class assemblies are designed and conducted by students. Each class section during the school year gets the opportunity to showcase their production for parents and peers, building their confidence and self-esteem.

Children learn better when they are taught in their ways and the ways that interest them the most.

All activities are an integral part of the curriculum and we aim to provide a plethora of experiences when exploring core subjects and beyond.

Academic Excellence

Readied to be global citizens, alive to a world of change and challenges

Our curriculum and methodology is designed to sharpen thinking processes by building strong and robust foundational skills. Students strive to enhance and apply their knowledge in a hands-on, meaningful way. This learning is not restricted to the classroom—it impacts their environment and societal interactions while building responsibility and initiative.

The curriculum emphasises the essential unity of knowledge and promotes an interdisciplinary approach to learning. We continually evaluate and modify our academic program to reflect the most advanced teaching methodologies, while providing students with opportunities to pursue their passions.

Children develop skills in all the basic subjects — English, Tamil, Mathematics, Social & Environmental Sciences, Computers, Physical Education, Art and Design — with the care and attention of dedicated educators, ensuring holistic development and a strong foundation in early years.

Our curriculum includes plenty of hands-on experiments where students learn through play, creativity, and engaging lessons. We encourage reading beyond the syllabus and provide regular exposure to life outside the classroom.

Foundation Classes

SBV’s Foundation course for IIT JEE stands as a transformative initiative poised to revolutionise school education. By offering this comprehensive programme, Sri Barathi Vidyalaya equips students with a strong foundation for competitive exams.

This initiative signifies a shift towards holistic education, fostering a community where academic excellence thrives. Through this programme, our school continues to redefine IIT-JEE preparation, paving the way for aspiring minds to achieve success.

We present a tailored in-school test prep programme designed to prepare students for diverse competitive exams like IIT-JEE, NEET, NTSE, and Olympiads. It seamlessly combines online and offline methodologies, maximising learning outcomes.

Key Highlights of the IIT-JEE Foundation Programme

The IIT-JEE Foundation programme stands out for its expert-led interactive lessons, tailored curriculum resources, AI-driven self-study tools, and national-level assessments mirroring real exam formats.

It also provides comprehensive student progress reports, continuous school support, and flexible programme versions catering to different learning needs.

  • Engaging sessions by expert instructors
  • Customised books & workbooks
  • AI-powered doubt-solving app
  • National-level assessments
  • Detailed progress tracking
  • Continuous year-round support

Lifelong learners reflect on their learning and apply it meaningfully. They also consider how their decisions impact society. With personal integrity and responsibility, they grow into global citizens—our ultimate goal.

Students as Teachers

Student Led Conferences

As a part of SLC, students become teachers for a day and explain a concept or give a short demonstration on a topic of their choice.

SLCs help in improving subject knowledge. Students can learn the most when they teach something. While participating in SLCs, students are motivated to understand concepts deeply in order to explain them better.

SLCs boost confidence, communication, and presentation skills. It’s not easy to speak in front of an audience, but these conferences help students overcome hesitation and grow into confident communicators.
