Why Us

Sri Barathi Vidyalaya – A unique educational institution, its aim is to impart learning that blends the traditional with the modern; the aesthetic with the scientific.

This vision of ushering in a new order in the sphere of education found emblematic representation in the school motto, “Piety. Justice. Wisdom.”

Today, a budding spirit characterizes the school’s activities, where the administration and teachers ensure that students keep up with the latest technological developments and global trends without losing touch with their roots.

Education at SBV School is a dynamic process that, through reflective and contextual pedagogy, takes students, teachers and parents on an engaging journey exploring intellectual, emotional and spiritual learning.

Joyful And Challenging. Why Not?

Numbers and letters can be taught, and we do so delightfully. But what about individuality?

Effective learning, both for academic and life skills, is stimulated and explained. It is experienced as moments that are spontaneous, yet thoughtfully orchestrated.

These experiences are drawn from a variety of sources, ensuring that learning remains engaging, meaningful and enjoyable for every child.
