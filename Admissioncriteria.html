Admission Criteria

The medium of instruction is English and the academic year for the school is from June to April.

Admissions are made in Pre-K.G., L.K.G., U.K.G. classes and from Standards 1 to X.

For direct admission, children should satisfy the age criteria:

  • 2+ years for Nursery
  • 4+ years for L.K.G.
  • 5+ years for U.K.G.
  • 6+ years for I Standard

Students from unrecognized institutions will not be admitted.
