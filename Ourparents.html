Our Parents

At Sri Barathi Vidyalaya, we firmly believe that education is a collaborative effort between teachers, students, and parents. As valued members of our school community, we extend a warm welcome to all parents.

With parents as our partners, we aspire to create global citizens who are innovative and possess a strong sense of values.

Role of Parents in Your Child's Education

Partners in Learning

We view parents as partners in their child’s educational journey. Your involvement, support, and encouragement play a vital role in shaping your child’s academic success and personal development.

Open Communication

We foster open communication between parents and teachers, ensuring transparency and collaboration. Whether it is discussing your child’s progress, addressing concerns, or sharing achievements, we welcome your input and feedback.

Engagement Opportunities

From parent-teacher conferences and school events to volunteering opportunities, there are numerous ways for parents to actively engage with our school community. Your participation enriches the educational experience for all students and strengthens our sense of community.

Caretakers

Parents are always welcome to contact the educators whenever they feel it is necessary.

Children, staff, and parents come together regularly to celebrate student accomplishments and discuss school happenings. It is a wonderful way to build a stronger school community while reinforcing human values.

For us, assessment is an integral part of teaching and must be formative. Educators continually assess their students using a variety of innovative strategies to inform planning and ensure that students’ learning needs are met.

Formal written reports are presented to parents.

Support for Parents

Parent Portal

Stay informed and connected with our secure Parent-Student App. Access important school announcements, your child’s grades, attendance records, and upcoming events with ease.

Support Services

Our school provides access to resources and support services to assist parents in navigating various challenges and concerns.

Whether you need academic guidance, counseling support, or assistance with special education services, we are here to help.

Engage, Connect, Thrive!

At Sri Barathi Vidyalaya, we recognize the invaluable contributions of parents in creating a nurturing and empowering learning environment for our students.

Together, let us foster a culture of collaboration, respect, and excellence as we support each child’s journey toward success.

For more information on parent resources, involvement opportunities, or to get in touch with our parent liaison, please visit our school or contact the school office.

We look forward to partnering with you in your child’s educational journey.
