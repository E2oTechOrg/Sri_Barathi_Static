Conceptual Understanding through Multimodal Learning
Smart Classrooms

All our classrooms are equipped with state-of-the-art technology that helps in teaching and learning at pace with global standards. All our classrooms are Smart TV enabled for audio-video learning. This provides better visualization to students providing robust conceptual clarity that is extremely critical for holistic learning.

Grade-wise activity kits

Learning by doing is something we attempt to inculcate in all our students. This is beautifully supported by the custom grade-wise activity kits. When students learn concepts hands-on, they are proven to have better understanding and retention.

Subject Specific Pedagogies

We follow subject-specific pedagogy for deeper conceptual understanding.

English

At Sri Barathi Vidyalaya, students don’t learn English as a subject, they learn it as a skill. It is ensured that a child learns the language at their own pace. A child’s learning is accelerated by 1.5x than conventional methods.

Maths

Let’s make Mathematics simple for your child. Math often seems abstract, but when connected to real life, it becomes enjoyable and engaging. At Sri Barathi Vidyalaya, Math is taught using the Concrete – Pictorial – Abstract approach for better understanding and retention.

EVS and Science

At Sri Barathi Vidyalaya, we ensure students become independent thinkers by helping them gain first-hand understanding of concepts through:

• Exploring
• Experimenting
• Observing things around them

Our learning by doing approach helps children reflect and retain concepts.

School Learning connected with Home Learning

Gamified Learning

Learning doesn’t have to be a tedious, boring task. On the Student App we provide, your child gets access to extensive practice tests and more that make learning fun. They can take part in quizzes and interactive activities that ensure continuous growth.

Become a part of your child’s learning journey

The Student App has an exclusive parent section where you can directly monitor, assess, and celebrate your child’s learnings and achievements. You can also track attendance and report cards easily.
