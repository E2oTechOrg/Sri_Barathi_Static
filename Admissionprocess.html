Admission Process

Home - Admission Process

Step 1
Enquiry For Admission

You can submit an online enquiry form or visit the school’s admissions office. Our counselor will contact you with the required information.

Step 2
Meet The Counselor

Our counselor will guide you through the process and explain how the school benefits your child.

Step 3
Campus Tour

Visit the campus to understand the school environment, facilities, and culture.

Step 4
Registration

Complete registration by visiting the admission office and filling out the form.

Step 5
Assessment

Students will undergo an oral or written assessment for proper evaluation.

Step 6
Admission Confirmation

Admission is confirmed after completing all steps and paying the required fees.

Step 7
Parent Orientation Program

Parents attend an orientation program to understand the school’s vision, mission and ecosystem.
